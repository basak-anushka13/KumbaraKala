KumbaraKala

An Android application developed using Kotlin and Firebase Firestore to digitally promote traditional clay products and support local artisans.


Download APK

Project Features

Clay Product Catalog

Displays traditional clay products in a beautiful gallery-style interface.

Firebase Integration

Stores and retrieves product and user-generated data dynamically.

Benefit Card Generation

Generates personalized digital cards using Canvas API.

WhatsApp Sharing

Allows users to share generated benefit cards securely.

Technology Stack

Kotlin • Android Studio • XML • Firebase Firestore • RecyclerView • CardView • Canvas API • FileProvider • Generative AI

Problem Statement

Traditional potters and artisans are losing business due to increasing use of plastic and steel products. KumbaraKala solves this problem by digitally promoting eco-friendly clay products and generating personalized digital benefit cards for awareness and marketing.
